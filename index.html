New Intervention · Dormant & Uninstalled Users

Let them win first. Ask later.

These users aren't coming back because we ask.
They'll come back if we let them win something first, somewhere easy — then put the reward behind the app.

Tambola is a great fit: 50% of users who start a game finish it. 80% of users who hit their first win play it all the way through.

People don't walk away from a game they're already winning.

The Flow

Play → Win → Login → Win → Install.

STEP 1
Just let them in
Click ad → land on Tambola → play instantly. No login. No OTP.
STEP 2
First win → login
A wall asks them to log in to keep playing. They've already felt the win — the ask doesn't feel like a tax.
STEP 3
Login, then back in
Progress carries over. Same ticket. Same win. No reset.
STEP 4
Second win → install
Install wall fires. They've won twice. Installing feels like claiming what they earned.
