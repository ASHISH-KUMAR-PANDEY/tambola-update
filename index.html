01 · Why

Why Live Tambola.

Content alone doesn't bring dormant users back. We needed a reason for them to open the app — a reason that repeats, rewards, and travels.

The problem

Users don't return for content they can watch anywhere. Notifications don't pull them back. Free content pulls them in but doesn't convert.

The bet

A weekly appointment creates anticipation. Winning creates stickiness. Cash prizes create urgency. Put them together — and you get a repeatable habit loop that spills into content consumption.

02 · How it works

The format.

A three-layer engagement system. Live on Sunday, solo every day, a passive weekly grind for those who can't commit to an hour.

Sunday Live
Live Tambola
8 PM Sunday. YouTube-hosted live draws. 5 prize categories: First 5, Top Line, Middle Line, Bottom Line, Full House. Cash prizes for every category.
Daily
Solo Tambola
New game daily. Same 5 categories. Leaderboard of fastest winners across the week. Winners recognised in the Sunday legends section.
New · Weekly
Individual Game
15 numbers drip in every day, Monday to Sunday. Mark at your own pace. No time pressure. Built for passive, low-effort return.

Rewards range from cash prizes for Sunday winners to subscription extensions and recognition for top solo players.
